LAGOS: My City, My Home

Hi, my name is Kolawole Keshinro, and I am a proud Nigerian. I grew up in the city of Lagos. Lagos, Nigeria, is often referred to as the commercial heartbeat of the Nigerian economy. The coastal city was named by Portuguese traders in the 14th century.

Lagos is a beautiful place located in the western part of Nigeria, with a population of about 20 million. This is why it is known as the commercial nerve of the Nigerian economy.
